  1. 5 Deadly Venoms

    5 Deadly Venoms was founded in January 2018 on three core principles: community, charity, and love for New York. 5DV has been the original NYXL supporters since day 1. Between watchparties for our favorite team and casual meetups in the off-season, the crew supports local at-need neighbors through fundraising and clothing drives. Fully community-led and based in the Lower East Side, 5DV has welcomed friends from all over the world.

    University Settlement

    Lead. Act. Impact.

    The 5 Deadly Venoms are proud to partner with University Settlement, one of New York's most dynamic social service institutions that has been around for over 130 years. University Settlement supports over 40,000 New Yorkers across all five boroughs through a variety of programs including adult literacy, youth support, senior programs, mental health services, and more.
